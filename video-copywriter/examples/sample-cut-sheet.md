## Cut Guidance

### General Rules
- Cut all pauses > 0.5s (unless marked [PAUSE] for dramatic effect)
- Remove all filler words ("um", "uh", "like", "you know")
- Jump cut mid-sentence for energy at: 0:04, 0:08, 2:00, 3:30
- Maintain eye contact with camera throughout (crop if needed)

### Section-Specific

**Hook (0:00-0:12)**
- Rapid cuts, no breaths between statements
- Each sentence = new take/angle if possible
- End on hold for "everything changes about how you use it"

**Librarian Analogy (0:35-1:45)**
- Slower pacing, let the analogy breathe
- B-roll covers any delivery imperfections
- Return to face for key moments: "That's it. That's AI."

**Why This Matters (2:00-3:30)**
- Medium pace, conversational feel
- Cut away to B-roll on "frustrated" mentions
- Stay on face for insights

**Better Input Example (3:45-5:00)**
- Can use text overlays for the two question comparisons
- Show the contrast visually
- Energy builds through section

**CTA (6:15-6:45)**
- Single take preferred, no cuts
- Tighter frame, more intimate
- Let final "See you there" land without cut
